NBU BUSINESS SCHOOL

VISION STATEMENT

The Vision of the Nigerian British University is to be a world-class British University in Nigeria, anchored on academic excellence and distinguished by a robust interface between research and industry.

MISSION STATEMENT
The mission of Nigerian British University is to develop globally competitive graduates by offering proactive and distinctive research opportunities, a learning and professional mentoring environment with adequate international collaborations, as well as commitment to vibrant industrial engagement to ensure practical and relevant academic experiences.

PHILOSOPHY
The Philosophy of the Nigerian British University is based on its core values of mentorship, academic dynamism, professional integrity, equity, and diversity. These values aim to inspire self-motivation and excellence as a prerequisite for building an enviable professional career.












In line with the NBU Vision and Mission Statements above, the Mission and Vision of NBU Business School are as follows:
                      Our Vision
To be a globally recognized business school, fostering innovative leaders who transform businesses and ethical practices for the betterment of the society.
                         Our mission
Our mission is to provide world-class business education and research that empowers students, supports economic development, and engages with communities to promote responsible and inclusive leadership.

Our Objectives
To continuously update the technical skills and competences of students through quality teaching, research and practical learning in order to enhance productivity, wealth maximization and social responsiveness.

INTRODUCTION
NBU Business School is a university-based Business School that offers a wide range of professional and academic programs in the field of business.
The academics at NBU Business School are characterized by a combination of rigorous coursework, practical teaching and research, experiential learning opportunities, and collaboration with faculty (Professors, Associate Professors, Senior Academics and PhD Students) and industry professionals. The school emphasizes an interdisciplinary and global approach to business education, preparing students for success in the rapidly evolving global business environment.
Moreover, the school offers numerous resources for career development, including internship opportunities, mentorship programs, and networking events. This focus on practical application and professional development ensures that students are well-prepared for their future careers.
Overall, the academics at NBU Business School aim to foster intellectual curiosity, innovation, and ethical decision-making, providing students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the business world.

DIPLOMA SHORTCOURSES FLOATED IN NBU BUSINESS SCHOOL
INTRODUCTION
The contemporary courses being offered by Nigerian British University Business School are structured not only to be top-notch but also to be highly sought-after courses that are capable of developing new practical knowledge, innovative thinking, creativity, skills and abilities in the participants.
Before designing these short courses, the following factors have been taken into consideration: the urgent need to develop and sustain indigenous business school, the nature and economic trend, the products and services that are dominating the Nigerian economic landscape such as oil and gas. Also, considered necessary are the need to identify trade associations and satisfy their peculiar needs, the possibility of fostering collaborative engagements, the level of competitiveness from other business schools.
In particular, the short courses are designed to reflect and fill the skills-gaps and relevant practical knowledge that are needed and the professional and expert opinion in the relevant industries. They are intended to empower students with the latest technological and entrepreneurial skills. Essential focus is also directed towards identifying all the necessary practical business steps, various challenges constantly being faced by entrepreneurs in the course of their businesses so as to prepare participants to be ready solution providers in real world business situations.
Business Challenges are not impossible equation to solve or to surmount, they are meant to be identified well in advance and the latest relevant techniques need to be used to provide solution to them in order to take the businesses to the next level and help organizations achieve their goals.
The most practical objectives of these short courses are that they will develop expertise, proficiency, and critical thinking in the participants and students in order to enhance their value and ability to apply practical business concepts, analyze complex information, evaluate and identify the best alternative investment options, and make informed decision that will address real world business problems.
The courses will foster creativity, innovation, teamwork, build practical knowledge confidence and most importantly expose students to industry experts and make them readily employers of labour or employable by potential employers to become intrapreneurs who will take on entrepreneurial roles within an organization to drive growth, innovation and improvement within the relevant industry, whichever group one belongs.
To maintain standard and quality of the Diplomas and degrees, there shall be examinations properly established, administered and conducted to meet set guidelines of the NBU business School.
The proposed courses will be presented to the Board of NBU Business School for its consideration and when recommended by the board, they will be presented to the Senate of the university for approval. The Senate may also be required to approve courses at the level of PGD, Masters and PhD as list increases.

1. Diploma in Finance & Financial Reporting for Top Executives 
(3 Months)

1. Course Objectives
This intensive three-month diploma program equips top executives with the essential financial knowledge and skills to make informed strategic decisions and effectively oversee financial performance.

2. Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of the program, participants will be able to:
* Interpret and Analyze key financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement)
* Evaluate the financial health and performance of an organization
* Apply financial analysis tools to assess investment opportunities and risks
* Communicate effectively with financial professionals and interpret financial reports
* Make informed decisions regarding budgeting, resource allocation, and overall financial strategy
* Develop a critical understanding of corporate governance and financial reporting principles

3. Course Outline
Module 1: Financial Statements and Analysis (4 weeks)
* Understanding the Fundamental Financial Statements
* Ratio Analysis and Performance Evaluation
* Fina ncial Forecasting and Budgeting Techniques
* Cost Analysis and Management Accounting Fundamentals

Module 2: Investment Analysis and Capital Budgeting (4 weeks)
* Time Value of Money and Discounted Cash Flow Analysis
* Capital Budgeting Techniques (Net Present Value, Internal Rate of Return)
* Risk Management and Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM)
* Mergers and Acquisitions: Financial Evaluation and Due Diligence

Module 3: Corporate Finance and Financial Strategy (4 weeks)
* Cost of Capital and Capital Structure Decisions
* Dividend Policy and Shareholder Value Creation
* Working Capital Management and Cash Flow Optimization
* Financial Planning and Strategic Decision Making

Module 4: Financial Reporting and Corporate Governance (4 weeks)
* International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)
* Internal Controls and Risk Management Frameworks
* Corporate Governance Principles and Ethical Conduct
* Board Responsibilities and Financial Reporting Oversight

Module 5: Financial Markets and Economic Trends (3 weeks)
* Understanding the Financial System and Key Players
* Interest Rates, Inflation, and Foreign Exchange Risk
* Impact of Economic Conditions on Financial Performance
* Financial Literacy for Strategic Business Leadership




4. Who Should Attend?
This program is designed for busy top executives who require a strong foundation in financial principles without getting bogged down in technical details. It is ideal for:
* Chief Executive Officers (CEOs), Chief Operating Officers (COOs), and Chief Financial Officers (CFOs)
* Managing Directors, Business Unit Heads, and Senior Managers
* Non-Financial Executives seeking to enhance their financial literacy
* Board Members and Company Directors
* Entrepreneurs and Business Owners

By participating in this program, top executives can gain the financial acumen necessary to make strategic decisions with confidence, improve communication with financial teams, and ensure the long-term financial health of their organizations.

This intensive three-month diploma program equips participants with the fundamental knowledge, skills and abilities needed to understand what Desktop Publishing is all about in terms of the uncommon, overwhelming and unique characteristics as follows: (1) Understand the basics of desktop publishing and its applications (2) Learn the fundamental concepts of design, layout, and typography (3) Familiarize with desktop publishing software (e.g., Adobe InDesign, Microsoft Publisher) (4) Master the skills of creating and editing documents using desktop publishing software (5) Learn to design and layout text, images, and graphics

(9) Learn to visualize and communicate ideas effectively (10) Understand how to balance text and images in a document (11) Develop skills in creating visually appealing documents (12) Apply desktop publishing skills to create real-world documents (e.g., brochures, flyers, newsletters) (13) Learn to work with clients to understand their design needs (14) Develop project management skills to complete projects on time (15) Learn to troubleshoot common desktop publishing issues.
